How to Use This Calculator

Select your shape (slab, column, circular pad, or stairs), enter the dimensions in your preferred unit, then click Calculate. Adjust the quantity slider if you need multiple identical pieces and the waste percentage to account for spillage and overpour. Results appear instantly in cubic yards, feet, and meters.
