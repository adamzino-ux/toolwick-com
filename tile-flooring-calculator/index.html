How to Use This Tile Calculator

Enter your room's length and width, then input your tile dimensions and grout joint size. Adjust the waste percentage slider — use 10% for standard straight-lay patterns, 15% for offset/brick patterns, and 20–30% for diagonal or complex designs.

Add tiles per box and price per box to get instant cost estimates. Use the deductions field to subtract areas like islands, bathtubs, or built-in cabinets that won't be tiled.

The calculator rounds up to whole tiles and whole boxes, so you'll always have enough material to complete your project.
