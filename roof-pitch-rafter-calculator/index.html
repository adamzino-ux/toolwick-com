How to Use This Calculator

Enter your roof's horizontal run (half the building width) and vertical rise to instantly get the pitch ratio, rafter length, angle, and slope. You can also use the slider to explore different pitch ratios, or switch to "Pitch Ratio" or "Angle" input modes. Add an eave overhang for total rafter length including the overhang.

Results include a visual diagram and a detailed breakdown table. Toggle between feet and meters for metric projects.
