📖 How to Use This Calculator

Select your system voltage and safety factor, then add each appliance or device you want on the circuit — either by entering custom wattage or choosing from the presets. The calculator computes total amperage, recommends the correct circuit breaker size (following NEC 80% rule by default), and suggests the appropriate wire gauge.

The NEC (National Electrical Code) requires that continuous loads not exceed 80% of a breaker's rated ampacity. For a 20A breaker, the maximum continuous load should be 16A. This tool applies that rule automatically unless you change the safety factor.

Always consult a licensed electrician before performing electrical work. This tool is for estimation purposes only.
